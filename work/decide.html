Detailed description:

From Decide4AI I take with me the opportunity to have been able to touch many artificial intelligence tools and to have been able to make a big impact on many clients.

I was able to develop 6-month product demand forecasting model at product and shop level. This helped to better manage the company's stock.

I helped a digital newspaper with their data management and processing. This led us to develop an advertising model focused on people, with the aim of improving the CTR of the advertising shown. This project helped Decide4AI to continue with this company until the final solution was developed.

However, the best projects I did at Decide4AI were with a Spanish airline. For them I developed, together with their team, a destination recommendation model based on neural networks and a classification model. Thanks to the former, we managed to improve the CTR of the recommendations by 6%, which subsequently translated into more sales. With the latter, we were able to anticipate a person's flight motive to better manage their experience.
These last two projects were a major technical challenge, due to the implementation of the use of AWS in the team, as well as the improvement of the recommendation engine through a mixed classical and ML methodology. And the fine-tuning of the classification system so as not to misclassify people.
